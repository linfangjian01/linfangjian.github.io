Biography

I graduated from North China Electric Power University for my undergraduate degree and Xinjiang University for my master's degree (2016-2023). I have conducted research internships at Baidu Research, Shanghai AI Lab and Alibaba Group.

My research interests lie in the fields of deep learning, computer vision, and visual perception for autonomous driving. Specifically, I focus on 1) designing general-purpose models for various tasks (classification, detection, and segmentation); 2) developing multimodal architectures for large-scale models; and 3) exploring prompt engineering techniques. In addition, I am actively seeking a potential PhD advisor.
